Join SunShin Orchestra School

Welcome to SunShin Orchestra School, where we nurture the musical talents of middle and high school students. Our program offers a unique and enriching experience for young musicians to develop their skills, perform in ensembles, and grow as artists. Whether you are a beginner or an advanced player, our dedicated instructors will guide you through every step of your musical journey.

At SunShin Orchestra School, students have the opportunity to play a variety of instruments, participate in concerts, and join different orchestral groups tailored to their skill levels. Our goal is to foster a love for music and provide a supportive community where students can thrive. We also offer masterclasses, workshops, and opportunities to perform in prestigious events.
