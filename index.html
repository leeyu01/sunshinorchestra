Welcome to SunShin Orchestra Summer School

Sun Shin, a High School Orchestra Director on Long Island, is now offering a summer orchestra program. In 2024, Sun Shin's Orchestra received gold with distinction in level 6, the highest score in the highest level.

How this music program was created:

Sun Shin always wants her daughters to learn string instruments with the orchestra programs, just like other school districts with an orchestra program in Long Island. But the Queen's school district hasn't started the elementary and middle school orchestra program. Sun Shin teaches them the instruments and wants her kids to experience orchestra rehearsal and group lessons like in the Long Island school district. So, she decided to open this summer program. Her two daughters also will join the lesson group to learn instruments, have orchestra rehearsals, and have concerts.

Our orchestra summer program is for young students (grades 2-12). Four students will group each group lesson.
